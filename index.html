Lived Edutech Services

An edutech service provided to technical/non-technical institutions worldwide

LIVED EDUTECH SERVICES is an edutech service provided to technical/non-technical institutions worldwide. The objective of this eductech service is to conduct international conferences at various organizations as a co-organizer and create awareness and develop the knowledge of the students/researchers/academicians with the support of globally renowned professors/industrial experts. It also supports improving the quality of research articles published in various reputed publisher journals with the help of relative industry experts/scientists as a consultancy service. Edutech Services is a platform to conduct training sessions for students/researchers/academicians with the support of renowned subject/industry experts in hybrid mode.
