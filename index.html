LIVED EDUTECH SERVICES is an edutech service provided to technical/non-technical institutions worldwide. It also supports improving the quality of research articles published in various reputed publisher journals with the help of relative industry experts/scientists as a consultancy service. Edutech Services is a platform to conduct training sessions for students/researchers/academicians with the support of renowned subject/industry experts in hybrid mode.

Shipping Services

Shipping Services

Products ordered through the website for delivery within India are shipped through our courier partner if the delivery service to the destination is available. In the absence of a courier service to the desired location, the products are shipped through the Indian Postal Service.

Shipping to locations outside India is done through Indian Postal Service/DTDC/DHL International Couriers. International Book Post shipping requires us to ship books in open envelopes and is not registered. Therefore, we will not be in a position to provide a tracking number.

The shipping time mentioned anywhere on the website is approximate and we cannot guarantee them. Customers are recommended to place an order early on if they have a deadline to meet. Apart from the shipping time, the print on demand books also need time for production, which varies from 3 days to 2 weeks depending on the production requirement and quantity ordered.
